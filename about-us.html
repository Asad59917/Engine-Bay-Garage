Our History

"Engine Bay Garage was established in 2001 with a clear mission: to deliver reliable, convenient, and professional car repair services directly to our customers’ homes or workplaces. Starting as a small team of passionate technicians, we’ve grown into a trusted name across Dubai, known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Over the years, we've embraced innovation and mobile servicing solutions to keep up with the evolving automotive industry. Whether it’s routine maintenance or complex repairs, our skilled technicians continue to uphold the highest standards of care, making vehicle servicing stress-free and efficient for every customer. From humble beginnings working out of a single van, we’ve expanded to a full fleet of mobile service units equipped with advanced diagnostic tools and genuine spare parts. This mobility allows us to bring the garage to your doorstep—saving you time without compromising on quality."
